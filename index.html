Who am I?

Sina(like dinner in British) -no middle name- Behmanesh, born on 4 July 1999 (24 yo) in Garmsar little town in Semnan province in Iran. when I was 6, my parents bought me a PC. it had Windows XP 2000 on it. good old days. started to learn how to work with computers, installing video games, watching cartoons on my own. I could not do anything else but play video games, the PC became my best friend!
Never a good student, always a computer guy. In high school 3rd grade I told my Computer teacher that I want to do some programming, then he gave me a visual basic Microsoft book to study, people think it was a curse, but I think it was a luxury! In late 2016 I was introduced to Dota2, an online video game, it was a curse :D now (7/19/2023) I have played more than 5000 hours of Dota2. I could become an elite air force pilot at that time. In 2017 I went to university, Semnan university (please do not ask how I got accepted to be at Semnan University if I was a terrible student, maybe I explain it one day) to study software engineering (similar to computer science). passed the software courses like data structure, OOP programming, databases and etc. after 4 terms I decided to quit university and I did it, wanted to start my professional work, when I was 19 I relocated to Tehran for my first work as a junior engineer. it was Fun!
